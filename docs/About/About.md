#### Flock - move together, find your people

##### 1. About the App: 
	Flock is a hyperlocal social discovery platform designed to help people build real-world connections through shared interests, recurring activities, and local community events.

In a world where moving cities, changing routines, or simply growing older can make meeting new people surprisingly difficult, Flock makes discovering meaningful social experiences simple, comfortable, and accessible.

Whether you're new to a city, looking to expand your circle beyond college or work, rebuilding your social life, or trying to find people who enjoy the same hobbies as you. Flock helps you connect with communities that feel natural, relevant, and welcoming.

##### 2. Why Flock Exists :

Modern social platforms are optimized for endless scrolling, not genuine connection.
Many people today struggle with:
●	Moving to unfamiliar cities without knowing anyone
●	Staying trapped inside small social bubbles
●	Wanting to pursue hobbies without a community
●	Feeling isolated after major life changes
●	Finding local events that actually match their interests and age group

Flock was built to solve that.

Instead of random networking or dating-app style interactions, Flock focuses on low-pressure, interest-driven group experiences that help people meet organically.

##### 3. What Makes Flock Different:

Interest-First Discovery : People connect through activities and communities, not endless profiles.

Hyperlocal Experiences : Discover events, meetups, and communities happening nearby in real time.

Comfortable Social Matching : Find groups that align with your age range, interests, vibe, and experience level.

Recurring Communities : Not just one-time events — Flock encourages consistent local communities and long-term friendships.

Built for Real Life : Flock is designed to move people from online discovery to offline interaction as naturally as possible.

##### 4. User Scenarios:

1. New City, No Friends : 
A 22-year-old moves to Bangalore for their first job. Flock shows nearby meetups — a board game night, a weekend trek group, a tech networking coffee — filtered to their age range so they're not the youngest in the room by 15 years.

2. College Student, Wants More Than Campus : 
A 20-year-old wants to meet people outside their college bubble. Flock connects them to interest-based local groups (photography walks, startup chats, open mics) happening within 5km.

3. Hobby Without a Crew :  
Someone wants to play football every Sunday but has no regular group. Flock lets them join or create recurring hobby meetups and build a consistent local squad over time.

4. Post-Breakup / Social Reset : 
A 24-year-old comes out of a long relationship and wants to rebuild their social life. Flock offers low-pressure, group-format meetups — no dating app energy, just real shared-interest hangouts.

5. Event Organizer / Community Builder : 
A 23-year-old regularly hosts sketch comedy open mics and wants a reliable way to attract the right crowd. Flock gives them a platform to post, grow attendance, and build a loyal local following.
